# RentStrike AI — Business Plan & PRD

## Market
- **TAM:** 44M US rental households; $500B annual rent market.
- **SAM:** 5M households with active habitability disputes.
- **SOM Year 1:** 7,200 users = $208,800 ARR.

## Revenue: $29 Flat Fee
- Unit Economics: Revenue $29, Stripe $1.15, LLM $0.30, **Margin: $27.55 (95%)**.

## PRD — Feature Matrix
### ✅ Built
- Landing Page & $29 Stripe Checkout.
- Habitability Notice RAG Generator (Notice to Landlord, Code Enforcement, Rights Summary).

### ⬜ Planned
- Photo upload analysis (AI rates the severity of the mold/damage).
- Automated submission to local 311/Code Enforcement.
- Escrow account setup instructions (state-specific).

## Legal Framework
- **Implied Warranty of Habitability**: Established in Green v. Superior Court (CA) and similar cases in 49 states.
- **Retaliatory Eviction Statutes**: Protects tenants who report code violations.

## Roadmap
- **Phase 1 ✅:** MVP.
- **Phase 2:** Photo-based damage assessment.
- **Phase 3:** Integration with local Housing Authorities.
