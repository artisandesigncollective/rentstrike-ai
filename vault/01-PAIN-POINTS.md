# RentStrike AI — Pain Points

## Primary Pain Point
> **"My apartment has no heat/mold/roaches. I've told my landlord 3 times and they won't fix it. I'm paying $2,000/mo for a dump and I'm scared I'll be evicted if I stop paying."**

### 1. Habitability Violations
- **Problem:** Landlords ignore essential repairs (heat, water, structural) because they think tenants don't know the law.
- **Scale:** 11M+ Americans live in "substandard" housing conditions.

### 2. Eviction Fear
- **Problem:** Tenants don't know about "Retaliatory Eviction" protection.
- **Our Solution:** AI notice includes specific warnings about retaliatory eviction statutes, creating a legal shield for the tenant.

### 3. Legal Jargon
- **Problem:** "Implied Warranty of Habitability" sounds complex.
- **Our Solution:** We break it down into "Fix it or I'm taking $X out of next month's rent" in plain, legally binding English.

## User Personas
- **"The Low-Income Renter"**: Trapped in poor conditions because they can't afford a new deposit.
- **"The Urban Professional"**: Paying premium rent for a "luxury" apartment that has a broken elevator/HVAC for 3 months.

## Current Alternatives
1. **Pay and suffer** (80%).
2. **Move** (10%) — Costs thousands in deposits/movers.
3. **Legal Aid**: Overwhelmed, 6-month waitlists. RentStrike is instant.
4. **Small Claims**: RentStrike's notice usually forces the repair in 7 days, avoiding court.
